# Attribution and provenance

## JMdict

This project uses the JMdict Japanese multilingual dictionary as source
material. JMdict is maintained by the Electronic Dictionary Research and
Development Group (EDRDG) and its contributors.

- Project: https://www.edrdg.org/wiki/index.php/JMdict-EDICT_Dictionary_Project
- Licence: https://www.edrdg.org/edrdg/licence.html
- Download: https://www.edrdg.org/pub/Nihongo/JMdict_e.gz

The source Japanese forms, readings, English glosses, and Russian glosses are
not original work of this project. Ukrainian glosses are adaptations created
by this project's contributors. Every generated catalog records the source URL
and retrieval time so releases can be traced to an upstream snapshot.

No Warodai definitions or other non-redistributable dictionary content may be
copied into this repository.
